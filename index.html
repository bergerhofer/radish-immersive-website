We are Radish

At Radish, we believe technology isn't just a tool — it's a canvas for stories, emotions, and change. Design and code belong together, not just to make things work, but to make them feel alive, intuitive, and unforgettable.

Our studio is a playground where artists and technologists team up to bend reality, test limits, and bring bold ideas to life. From immersive web worlds to interactive installations, we craft experiences that spark curiosity, shift perspectives, and stay with people long after the moment has passed.

Every project is more than a deliverable — it's an experiment in possibility, a chance to flip expectations, move people, and grow something extraordinary.
